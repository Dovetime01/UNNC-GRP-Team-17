## What You Will Find Here

This open educational resource is organized around four topics:

**VP Workflow**  
Where you can understand VP work from the perspective of Unreal Engine operations, shot-based decision making, AI-assisted planning, AI asset generation, and version control with GitHub and Perforce.

**UE Basics**  
Foundational Unreal Engine skills for virtual production: scene organization and naming conventions, Blender-to-UE asset pipeline, and Level Snapshot as the unit of scene packaging and handoff.

**On-site Experience**  
Gathering the insights our project team gained through hands-on experience — from emergent on-set modifications and real-time asset generation, to virtual lighting systems and multi-shot light management in Unreal Engine.

**UE Plugin**  
A practical guide to how UE modules and plugins work, followed by a plugin development workflow using Visual Studio and Live Coding.
